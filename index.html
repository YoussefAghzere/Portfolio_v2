Educational Pathway

Primary School

Commencing my academic journey in primary school, I began with two delightful years in kindergarten under the tutelage of my teacher, Miss Zohra. Thereafter, I enrolled in School AlAmine, where I spent the initial two years of my primary education. Subsequently, I transferred to Dar AlKalam school, where I advanced my studies for the ensuing two years. Eventually, I concluded my primary schooling by joining AlKirawan school for the final two years. Notably, all of these scholastic experiences transpired within the precincts of Laayoune, a city situated in the southern region of Morocco.

Junior High School

Throughout my three-year tenure in junior high school, I exclusively attended Allal Ben Abdallah school in Laayoune city, the pearl of Moroccan Sahara. This epoch was notably significant for me as it marked the onset of my profound appreciation for mathematics. I underwent a final examination, which I sat for and obtained exceptionally commendable results, achieving a 'Very Good' grade.

High School

In my initial year of high school, I attended Hassan 2 school located in Laayoune. However, for the final two years of my high school education, I relocated to Rabat, the capital city of Morocco, following a sixteen-year residency in Laayoune. During these two years (As a mathematics' sciences option student), I undertook two examinations, namely the Regional exam in the first year of BAC and the National exam in the second year. My primary objective was to remain dedicated and diligent in my studies, with the aim of obtaining commendable results. Ultimately, my hard work paid off, and I accomplished my goal.

Preparatory Classes (CPGE)

Upon completing my Baccalaureate, I decided to pursue a CPGE program, with a focus on Mathematics and Physics. This academic journey proved to be the most challenging two years of my educational experience, as I delved deeply into the core principles of mathematics and physics. The program not only equipped me with academic knowledge but also instilled valuable life skills such as patience, diligence, humor, and camaraderie. I would like to express my gratitude to ' Ssi lCochambre', Haytam EL HAYANI, and all the students from the 2018 promotion of Salmane EL FARISSI CPGE center for their unwavering support and encouragement during this challenging period. I also obtained commendable grades in all the competition exams I undertook, both in Morocco and France. After much contemplation, I decided to embark on a new academic adventure in France.

Engineering School

My journey at ENSICAEN, a renowned engineering school in France, began with my enrollment in the computer science program. This presented an excellent opportunity for me to enhance my proficiency in both French and English languages. Additionally, I received comprehensive training in essential computer science disciplines, including programming languages, algorithmic, algebra, analysis, probability, and more. In my second year, I opted for the ePCS (ePayment & Cyber Security) option, which proved to be a challenging yet rewarding experience. After completing a rigorous academic year, I embarked on a four-month internship in Norway, which has been a valuable opportunity for me to apply my skills and gain practical experience in Artificial Intelligence.
